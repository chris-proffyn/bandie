# Bandie Build elements

These are the various different elements we will build for Bandie:

## Bandie Homepage 
— The public landing page that explains what Bandie is, who it is for, and routes users toward band creation or band discovery.

## Public Band Promotion 
— Public mini-sites that help bands showcase who they are, what they play, where they operate, and how they can be booked.

## Band Directory 
— A searchable public directory where event organisers can find bands by genre, location, price, rating and availability.

## Band Account, Workspace and Membership 
— The private band domain where approved members manage the band’s operational activity.

## Songs and Repertoire Management 
— A searchable working songbook for managing the band’s repertoire, readiness and song metadata.
## Song Folder / Song Workspace 
— A dedicated workspace for each song, containing files, notes, parts and rehearsal resources.
## Setlist Management 
— Tools for creating, reusing and tracking setlists for rehearsals, gigs and specific event types.
## Calendar and Availability Planning 
— A shared availability planner for rehearsals and potential gig dates.
## Gig Management 
— A structured area for managing confirmed and provisional gigs, logistics, setlists and post-gig history.
## Booking Enquiries 
— A workflow for event organisers to contact bands and for bands to manage inbound booking interest.
## Notifications and Activity 
— Cross-product updates, reminders, review tasks and activity feeds.
## Media, Links and External Content 
— A way to organise external music, video, social and reference links around bands, songs and gigs.
## Mobile / Performance Mode 
— Mobile-friendly views for rehearsals and gigs, including readable setlists and song resources.
## Administration and Platform Foundations 
— Core platform capabilities such as authentication, permissions, file storage, roles, search and audit history.
