What Bandie is

One place for the things bands usually scatter everywhere.

Bands already use YouTube, Spotify, Bandcamp, Google Drive, WhatsApp, tab sites and spreadsheets. Bandie does not replace all of that. It turns it into a structured band hub: songs, gigs, setlists, availability, promotion and decisions.

🎤

Promote the band

Create a clean public profile with your name, logo, bio, photos, videos, tracks, social links and booking contact.

📅

Organise the gig

Keep dates, venues, arrival times, availability, setlists, notes and gear checklists in one shared place.

🎸

Agree the songs

Build your repertoire with keys, links, parts, lyrics, tabs, videos, rehearsal recordings and band-approved notes.

For bands

Stop chasing everyone. Start sounding organised.

Bandie is for amateur bands, cover bands, originals bands, weekend musicians and anyone without formal management.

  • Create a simple public band page you can send to venues.
  • Track gigs, rehearsals and member availability.
  • Build votable setlists for specific events.
  • Store song notes and links to tabs, videos and files.
  • Give every member the same version of the plan.

For event organisers

Find bands that are ready to play.

Event organisers need to know quickly: what does the band sound like, where are they based, are they suitable, and how do I book them?

  • Browse bands by location, genre and event type.
  • Watch videos and listen to tracks from one clean profile.
  • Check set length, setup needs and contact details.
  • Shortlist suitable bands for pubs, parties, festivals and community events.
  • Send a booking enquiry without hunting through socials.

How it works

Bandie connects the band’s outside world to its inside plan.

Keep rich media and files where they already live. Use Bandie to make them useful: attached to songs, gigs, setlists and booking profiles.

Create the band

Add name, logo, location, genre, socials, videos, tracks and booking details.

Add the songs

Build a shared repertoire with keys, durations, notes and links to tabs, videos and files.

Plan the gig

Check availability, build a setlist, confirm timings and make sure everyone knows the plan.

Share the profile

Send organisers one clean Bandie link instead of a messy chain of messages and scattered links.

For bands who want the fun, not the admin.

Bandie helps amateur bands look credible, get organised and be ready for the next rehearsal, gig or booking enquiry.
